English Grammar Exercises

Exercise Set 1: Fill in the blanks

1. She _______ to school every day. (go/goes)
2. I _______ breakfast at 8 AM. (have/has)
3. They _______ playing football now. (is/are)
4. We _______ our homework yesterday. (do/did)
5. The sun _______ in the east. (rise/rises)

Exercise Set 2: Choose the correct word

1. He (is/are) my best friend.
2. I (like/likes) ice cream.
3. She (do/does) her homework in the evening.
4. The children (play/plays) in the park.
5. We (was/were) at the party yesterday.

Exercise Set 3: Rearrange the words to form a sentence

1. book / reading / I / a / am
2. to / going / school / is / she
3. plays / evening / in / football / he / the
4. is / mother / my / cooking / dinner
5. watching / we / are / movie / a

Exercise Set 4: Identify the verb in the sentence

1. She sings beautifully.
2. They are playing cricket.
3. I wrote a letter.
4. He drinks tea every morning.
5. We watched a movie last night.
